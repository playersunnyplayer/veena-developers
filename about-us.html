Who We are

About Us

Building the future

Since its inception in 1990, Veena Developers have successfully delivered Residential, Corporate and Industrial Spaces with the essence of modernisation. Our persistent aim is to offer high-quality construction while also providing amenities for the convenience of our customers and their luxurious lifestyle. We at Veena Developers have always believed that Integrity, Honesty and Trust are the pillars of our strength and we always strive to establish a long-term connection with our clients, one based on a solid foundation of trust. We want our customers to feel comfortable and secure as we assist them in realising their goal of having a home that is not only a mark of wealth but also a reflection of lifestyle. Our highly competent team of professionals perceive it as a mission to consistently work with a customer centric approach. With more than 30 years of real estate industry leadership, we believe that it is only the beginning for Veena Developers and that we have many more decades to demonstrate our endeavour to provide hassle-free customer experience in the sector.

Our vision

Our vision is to be the country's pioneering real estate service provider and the preferred name among real estate professionals. We diligently seek to achieve long-term client relationships through collaborative partnerships founded on Integrity and Harmony. As we grow, we will remain committed to upholding that which has served our business and clients well over the years.

Our mission

We are committed to accomplishing our vision by fostering an energising, productive, results-driven working environment fixated on providing superior quality services to customers and on-time delivery of projects. Our success is measured by the outcomes we achieve for our clients. Our ethics are based on our commitment to providing nothing but the best to our clients.
