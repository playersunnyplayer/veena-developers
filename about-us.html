Who We are

About Us

Building the future

Since its inception in 1990, Veena Developers have successfully delivered Residential, Corporate and Industrial Spaces with the essence of modernisation. Our persistent aim is to offer high-quality construction while also providing amenities for the convenience of our customers and their luxurious lifestyle.
We at Veena Developers have always believed that Integrity, Honesty and Trust are the pillars of our strength and we always strive to establish a long-term connection with our clients, one based on a solid foundation of trust. Our highly competent team of professionals perceive it as a mission to consistently work with a customer centric approach. We want our customers to feel comfortable and secure as we assist them in realising their goal of having a dream home that is not only a mark of their wealth but also a reflection of their lifestyle.
With Leadership of more than 30 years in the Real Estate industry, we believe that it is only the beginning for Veena Developers and that we have many more decades to demonstrate our endeavour to provide hassle-free customer experience in the Sector.
