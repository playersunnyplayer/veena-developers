Corporate Social Responsibility

Veena Developers has always believed in giving back to the Society and has Never missed out on any opportunity to help the people in need. From making donations to regularly feeding the voiceless animals, we consider it our responsibility to be the name of trust whenever someone is in need.
In 2014, the Charitable Trust 'Vanitaben Natvarlal Sanghavi Foundation' was established with the goal of empower and help the underprivileged and needy people. The trust is named after Shreemati Vanita ji, Honorable Mother of Shri Haresh Natvarlal Sanghavi, the trust ensures that donated funds are used for the intended purpose. Under this trust name Veena Developers do all the social activities on regular basis

Religious

Veena Developers understand the sentimental value of being associated with religion, hence as a way to reflect our respect for the religious beliefs, the following is carried out by Veena Developers.

  • Veena developers prioritize the ethos of the community as a whole and hence has been organizing numerous kathas at mahavir Nagar, Kandivali (W), Mumbai
  • As a part of its religious practices, Veena developers actively organises Kathas at Mahuva, District Bhavnagar, Gujarat
  • A beautifully constructed Govardhannathji Temple at Vasai.
  • Iskon Temple donation given on Janmashtami
Gavshala

Helping voiceless Animals is as important as any other good deed. Veena Developers make sure that holy Animal-Cows never meet a bad fate and are respectfully treated at Gavshala.

  • Veena Developers contributes to Cattle feeding on a monthly basis through the Allaram Sahayta charitable trust.
  • Cattle Feeding Donation in Mata Ji Gaushala Barsana, Uttar Pradesh
Social Activity

Whenever nation is in need or in any unexpected situation Veena Developers always stay ahead to help.

  • During the time of pandemic Veena developers Provided food & grocery kits to needy people from Virar, Vasai & Kandivali.
  • We provide Food Help to approx 100 needy families of Sanghavi samaj during the COVID period.
  • A considerable donation was made to the Prime Minister care fund.
  • MCHI – Relief fund
  • Kerala Flood Donation
Education

Veena Developers believe that an Educated Nation is a prosperous Nation. Giving a helping hand to underprivileged Children not only bring prosperity to the society but also brings pride and a hope of a better future.

  • Educated youth is the greatest asset to a nation, Veena developers Donated towards the education of students with an unsound financial background.
Medical

Hundreds of underprivileged families are not able to get even the basic medical attention, Veena Developers prioritize the well-being of these communities by various initiatives .

  • Veena developers Donated at Asha Parekh Hospital (Santacruz, Mumbai) for better medical facilities.
  • Considerable Medical donations at Hanumant Hospital (Mahuva, Gujarat)
  • Contributed in the development of Hitvardhak Hospital (Kandivali, Mumbai)
  • Veena developers help so many Individuals who require medical help
Donations

With the view to empower the underprivileged people, Veena Developers carry out the following charitable activities

  • Veena developers believe in giving more to the society and thus made considerable Donations to Allaram Sahayta charitable trust, Since May 2019 (Per month 54 kit of grocery & 54 tiffin each day to needy person)
  • Donations for Yamuna Seva Trust
